About Apply to Program Program Artists POAP Program

The Perpetual POAP Program (PPP) was funded by Lil Nouns to provide a fast-paced small grants route for artists to produce Nounish POAPs. The program funded artists for 7-months and garnered over 2000 mints. Below you can see some of the amazing art created by the PPP artists.
