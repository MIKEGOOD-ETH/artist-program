Welcome, Anon!
The Perpetual Artist Program provides small grants to artists creating artwork released directly into the public domain under the Creative Commons cc0 license. Eligible artists can receive funding up to 1 ETH. Participants are also awarded a Nounish pfp, symbolizing their contribution to the project and Nouns community. Interested in applying?
