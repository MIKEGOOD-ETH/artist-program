About the Program

The ArtistProgram is a small grants program funding artists to create artwork in various mediums. Artists voted into the program then contribute by voting for artists in upcoming funding rounds creating a perpetual arts program: for artists, by artists.

Funded by NounsDAO via Proposal 488.

How to Apply

Currently new members are only accepted through Open Rounds. A schedule of ongoing and upcoming rounds can be found on the “Open Rounds” page.
